How did it start

As the founder of Fit-IT, being a java developer (freelancer) myself I witnessed the pain points of companies finding good Java developers in Belgium and the payment of high costs once a good java developer is found.

The impact of the way of working due to the Covid was enormous and till today employees continue to keep that working module. So, does my team coming from different parts of Europe only meets once a week in the office.

When the war in Ukraine started at the beginning of this year (2022), Zelenski did a call on all companies to trade with Ukraine, so that the economy of Ukraine could stay strong. Men are not allowed to leave their country. I started some research and came across very highly qualified java developers in Ukraine but also other Ester European countries.

With a background in IT and account management it was always my dream to combine both backgrounds with the urge I felt to help Ukrainian people.

The idea of connecting Ukrainians for an affordable price to Belgium companies was born.
